## 2. Products — a growing catalogue, three income layers
Layer A — Electronic products: cheap, volume, no geography limit, no
per-sale time cost. The scalable core. Can extend beyond Slovakia.
Layer B — Live course (~once a month): mid price, limited to region and to
owner's time. Covers main monthly costs.
Layer C — Birth attendance (~EUR 500, ~once a month): highest price, most
personal, region-only, highly time-intensive and unpredictable.

Known products so far (examples, not final):
- Pre-birth checklist — free — lead magnet (PDF)
- ~1-hour birth-prep course — free — lead magnet (video, via Telegram)
- Partner training PDF — EUR 15 — electronic
- Live 7-hour seminar — EUR 60 — live format
- Birth attendance — ~EUR 500 — live, region-only
