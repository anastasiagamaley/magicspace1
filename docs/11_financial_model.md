## 1. Market facts — Slovakia (verified, web search 2026-05-16)
- ~46,000 births in Slovakia in 2024 — lowest since 1993.
- ~3,800 births per month, country-wide.
- Birth numbers have fallen every year since 2018; decline projected to
  continue. The market is small AND shrinking.

## 2. Why 3,800/month is NOT the addressable market
From the country-wide figure, subtract layers:
- only those who buy paid online preparation (a minority);
- only those the ads actually reach;
- only those in the right pregnancy 'window' (near term);
- minus language (Slovak-only at launch excludes Russian-speakers).
Realistic monthly pool of potential buyers: tens, maybe ~100-150 with good
advertising. Of that pool, only a few % buy.

## 3. Three income layers (mark each: automatic vs. owner's time)
| Layer | Product | Price | Income type |
|---|---|---|---|
| A | Electronic products | low | AUTOMATIC — works without the owner |
| B | Live course (~1/month) | EUR 60 | OWNER'S TIME — region only |
| C | Birth attendance (~1/month) | ~EUR 500 | OWNER'S TIME — region, unpredictable |
Layer C is the OPPOSITE of the automation goal: births do not follow a
schedule (night, weekends, during the day job). Recorded so the owner sees
the cost of this income in hours of life, not just euros.

The Slovak market is small and shrinking. Inside Slovakia, bet on cheap
electronic products by volume (the owner's own instinct — confirmed by the
