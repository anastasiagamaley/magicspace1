## 1. Competition moved, it did not vanish
Electronic products do NOT compete with other doulas. They compete for
ATTENTION against FREE content: YouTube, Facebook groups, articles, apps.
The buyer's question is 'why pay EUR 15 when free content exists'.

## 2. Differentiation
The answer is product quality and specificity — a product noticeably more
useful and concrete than free content. This is authoring work; automation
does not do it.

## 3. Why earlier ads may not have worked — diagnosis needed
`[ ] DECISION:` Determine WHY previous advertising did not work before
automating any advertising. Possible causes: weak/absent landing page;
wrong offer; wrong audience or ad copy. Automation does not fix a broken
offer — it just spends budget faster. Find a working chain
(ad -> landing -> signup -> sale) on a small EUR 50-100 test FIRST, then
automate it.
